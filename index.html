Our Story

Two Mates.
One Mission.

We're two 13-year-olds from the Blue Mountains who got tired of seeing overgrown lawns in our neighbourhood — and decided to do something about it.

What started as helping out nearby turned into a proper business. We realised people want lawn mowing that's reliable, affordable, and done right — so we built RapidCut from the ground up ourselves. Website and all.

We're not a big company. We're local kids who show up when we say we will, work hard, and take pride in every single lawn we mow. No shortcuts. No excuses.

Thanks for supporting a small local business — it genuinely means a lot. 💚
